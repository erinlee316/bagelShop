About Me

Hello! I'm Erin Lee, a student at Diablo Valley College majoring in data science, and I am currently applying to transfer to a 4-year university. One of my passions is baking, so I created this website to share my creations with you! All of the items found on this page are self-made, including the recipe. I have also altered the recipe to be dairy-free, but please look at the ingredients list to clarify potential allergens before ordering, as some of the bagels may contain other allergen products. Explore my website and order delicious bagels for delivery or pick-up bi-monthly!
