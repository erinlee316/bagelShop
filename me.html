About Me

Hello, I'm Erin Lee! I am a student at Diablo Valley College who is majoring in data science. I started this bagel business in 2022. At that time, we were beginning to return to normal pre-pandemic life, so I decided to utilize my passion for baking in the form of a business to reconnect with my community.

For the past year, this passion project has served as a creative outlet, combining my love of trying exotic cuisines and dexerity skills to create dozens of bagels. I'm so excited to bring you along my journey. Add energy into your daily life with a bite of my bagel!
