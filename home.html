The Beginning

Welcome to Erin's Bagel Shop - where passion meet dough! I've been creating sweet treats since childhood, turning my lifelong love for baking into a small business. Here, each bagel is mixed, shaped, and baked with care, bringing you my authentic bagel recipes. From seasonal varieties to innovative flavors, my bagels are sure to have you coming back with each order.

Indulge in the crisp exterior and pillowy interior of my bagels, made with quality ingredients. Whether it's for a family breakfast or a brunch with friends, my diverse menu promises a flavor for every palate.

Start Your Bagel Journey

Ready to experience the bliss of a perfect bagel? Browse my website, place your order, and get excited to indulge in delicious bagels for delivery or pick-up bi-monthly!

**Please look through the ingredients lists found in the menu page to clarify potential allergens before ordering, as some of the bagels may contain allergen products. It is possible to order gluten-free bagels. Just add in the notes section that you prefer your bagels to be gluten-free prior to submitting the order form.
