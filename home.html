THE BEGINNING

Welcome to Erin's Bagel Shop - where passion meet dough! I've been created sweet treats since childhood, turning my lifelong love for baking into a small business. Here, each bagel is mixed, shaped, and baked with care, bringing you my authentic bagel recipes. From seasonal varieties to innovative flavors, my bagels are sure to have you coming back with each order.

Indulge in the crisp exterior and pillowy interior of my bagels, made with quality ingredients. Whether you're a first-time bagel orderer or a frequent buyer, my diverse menu promises a flavor for every palate.

IMPORTANT

Please look through the ingredients lists found in the menu page to clarify potential allergens before ordering, as some of the bagels may contain allergen products. It is possible to order gluten-free bagels. Add in the notes if you prefer your order to be gluten-free prior to submitting the order form.
